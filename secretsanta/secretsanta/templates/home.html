What is Secret Santa?

Secret Santa is a fun gift exchange where people draw a random name to decide who they'll be buying a gift for. The catch is, it's all kept a secret until the gift exchange happens. Participants usually give hints or wish lists to help the gift giver choose something thoughtful. The big reveal takes place at a get-together, making it a simple and enjoyable way for friends, family, or coworkers to share the joy of giving during the holidays.
